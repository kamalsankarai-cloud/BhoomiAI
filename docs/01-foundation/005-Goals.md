These goals guide planning, implementation, and future evaluation of the platform.

# 3. Strategic Goals

## SG-01 – Build a Unified Agriculture Platform

Develop one integrated platform for managing agricultural operations instead of multiple disconnected tools.

---

## SG-02 – AI-First Platform

Embed AI into every major module to improve productivity and decision support.

---

## SG-03 – Scalable Architecture

Support growth from:

- One farmer
- One farm
- Multiple farms
- Agricultural companies
- SaaS platform

---

## SG-04 – Documentation First

Maintain complete documentation before implementation to improve quality and long-term maintainability.

# 6. AI Goals

BhoomiAI will:

- Provide intelligent recommendations.
- Automate repetitive tasks.
- Support natural language interactions.
- Build a searchable knowledge base.
- Generate analytics and forecasts.
- Assist, not replace, user decisions.

---

# 7. Module Goals

Each module should:

- Solve a specific business problem.
- Reuse platform services.
- Expose APIs.
- Support audit logging.
- Support attachments and comments where relevant.
- Allow future expansion.

# 9. Non-Goals (Version 1)

The first release does not aim to:

- Replace enterprise ERP systems.
- Provide autonomous farming.
- Support every agriculture sector.
- Build a public marketplace.

These may be considered in future versions.
