# 1. Purpose

This document defines the boundaries of the BhoomiAI platform.

It clearly identifies:

- What the project will include.
- What is intentionally excluded.
- Future expansion areas.

This helps control scope and prevents unnecessary changes during development.

---

# 2. Project Scope

BhoomiAI will provide a unified platform for managing agricultural operations through configurable modules, intelligent automation, AI assistance, and centralized data management.

The platform will initially focus on supporting personal and family-owned farms while maintaining an architecture that can expand to commercial farms and SaaS deployments.

# 4. Out of Scope (Version 1)

The following are intentionally excluded:

- Autonomous tractors
- Drone flight control
- Marketplace
- E-commerce
- Blockchain
- Advanced satellite analytics
- Government subsidy management
- Multi-country taxation

These features may be added in future versions.

# 7. Platform Boundaries

BhoomiAI focuses on operational management and decision support.

The platform is not intended to replace specialized accounting software, ERP systems, or laboratory information systems in Version 1.

Instead, it should integrate with them where appropriate.

---

# 8. Scope Management

Any proposed feature outside this document should:

1. Be documented.
2. Undergo business review.
3. Assess technical impact.
4. Update the roadmap if approved.
5. Receive approval before implementation.
