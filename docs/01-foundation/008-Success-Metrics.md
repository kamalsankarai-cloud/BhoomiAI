# 1. Purpose

This document defines the measurable indicators used to evaluate the success of BhoomiAI.

Success metrics help determine whether the platform delivers value to users and meets its business and technical objectives.

---

# 2. Measurement Principles

Every metric should be:

- Specific
- Measurable
- Achievable
- Relevant
- Time-bound (SMART)

Metrics will be reviewed periodically and refined as the platform evolves.
